ABOUT US

SOH MELBOURNE is a fragrance house creating bespoke candles, perfumes, and room sprays that celebrate modern Australian elegance. Founded in 2013 by husband and wife duo Nathan and Kristine Brown, every product is proudly hand-poured and crafted in Melbourne. Driven by Kristine's vision and the expertise of renowned perfumer François Merle-Baudoin, SOH brings together artistry, simplicity, and sophistication to design scents that capture purity, freshness, and a connection to nature.
